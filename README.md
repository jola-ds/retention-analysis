# KKBox Subscription Survival Analysis

A comprehensive analysis examining customer churn patterns in the KKBox music streaming service using cohort analysis and survival analysis (Kaplan-Meier estimators and Cox Proportional Hazards regression).

## Overview

This project analyzes subscription data from KKBox (a music streaming service) to understand when and why customers churn. Using survival analysis techniques, we model time-to-churn and identify factors that influence customer retention.

### Business Questions To Address - Ongoing

- When are customers most likely to churn?
- How long do subscriptions typically last?
- Which factors (pricing, auto-renewal, subscription history) affect churn risk?
- How do different customer segments compare in retention?
- What is the impact of long-term subscription plans vs. monthly plans?
